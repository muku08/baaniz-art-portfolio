Commission Terms & Conditions

Welcome! To ensure we create the best possible artwork for your project, please read through these simple terms before we get started. By commissioning a project, you agree to the following workflow and refund policies.

1. Project Kickoff & Your Vision

Great art starts with a great brief. To get the best results—whether it is a logo, a custom PFP, or a sticker pack—I need to know what is in your head.

  • Clear Ideas: You must provide a clear, detailed description of what you are looking for before work begins.
  • References: If you have any mood boards, sketches, color palettes, or reference images, please share them upfront! The more visual context you provide, the closer the first draft will be to your vision.

2. Communication & Feedback

Collaboration is key to this process. Once I start working on your project, I will send you rough sketches and work-in-progress samples.

  • Timely Responses: You are expected to provide regular, constructive feedback on the samples provided.
  • Revisions: Prompt feedback allows me to make adjustments early in the process and ensures your final assets are delivered on time.

3. The 80/20 Refund Policy (The "Kill Fee")

Sometimes, despite our best efforts, a project just doesn't click. If we go through the sampling and feedback process but ultimately cannot come to a mutual conclusion on the final artistic direction, we will close the project.

  • The Refund: In this scenario, you will receive an 80% refund of your total payment.
  • The Deduction: The remaining 20% is retained as a non-refundable fee to cover the time, labor, and creative effort already spent conceptualizing and drafting your initial samples.
